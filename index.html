Learn About Us

Redsea Barber Shop is a full-service barber shop offering the best in hair care services for men. We are located in the heart of downtown Calgary and have been serving the community for over 4 years. Our team of highly skilled and experienced barbers are dedicated to providing you with the highest level of customer service. We offer a wide range of services including haircuts, shaving, beard trimming, and much more. We use only the highest quality products and take pride in our work. We believe that every man deserves to look his best and feel confident. That's why we offer affordable prices and a relaxed, comfortable environment. Stop by Redsea Barber Shop today and let us help you look your best!
